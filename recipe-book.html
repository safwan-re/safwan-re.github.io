Ground Beef Curly Noodle

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef
Oriental flavored ramen noodles
Can diced tomatoes
Can whole kernel corn 		1 pound
1 (3 ounce) Package
1 (14.5 ounce)
1 (10 ounce)

Step 1: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Crumble in the ground beef, and cook until no longer pink, stirring frequently. Drain off grease.
Step 2: Stir in the flavor packet from the noodles, tomatoes, and corn (with their juices). Break up the noodles slightly, and add them to the skillet. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes, or until noodles are tender. Stir occasionally to break up any clumps of noodles.

Easy Roasted Broccoli

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Broccoli
Olive oil
Salt and black pepper 		14 ounces
1 tablespoon
---------------

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Step 2: Cut broccoli florets from the stalk. Peel the stalk and slice into 1/4-inch slices. Mix florets and stem pieces with olive oil in a bowl and transfer to a baking sheet; season with salt and pepper.
Step 3: Roast in the preheated oven until broccoli is tender and lightly browned, about 18 minutes.

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
